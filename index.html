I'm Jaehyun Park, a doctoral student focusing on cybersecurity in the Department of Computer Science at The University of Texas at Dallas. I conduct research under the guidance of Dr. Chung Hwan Kim, focusing on the security of industrial control systems and robotic platforms.

I earned my M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Southern California (USC) in 2023, following a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Korea Aerospace University (KAU) in 2021. My passion for protecting physical systems led me to transition into cybersecurity research, with a focus on securing industrial control systems and robotic infrastructures.
